Lasagna

Description

One of the site's most popular recipes: lasagna, a dish with meat, tomato sauce, cheese, and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Cheeses
  • One Egg
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
